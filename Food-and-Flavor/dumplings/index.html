The Origin of Dumplings

Dumplings have been loved in China for over 1,800 years. They were first created by the doctor Zhang Zhongjing, who wrapped meat and herbs in dough to help villagers stay warm during a harsh winter. Over time, these little pockets of comfort became a symbol of reunion, luck, and warmth. Today, dumplings are shared during festivals and family moments—small bites filled with care and good wishes.
