The Origin of Milk tea

Milk tea has been a beloved part of Chinese and Taiwanese culture for decades. From traditional styles passed down through families to modern boba shops on every corner, it has become a symbol of comfort, youthfulness, and sweet little moments in life. Whether enjoyed with friends, during late-night talks, or as a treat to yourself, milk tea is more than a drink — it’s a feeling.
