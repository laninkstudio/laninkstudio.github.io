Payment

Secure Payment

At Lán Ink Studio, we want your purchase experience to be smooth, safe, and effortless.

We currently support two secure payment methods.


Gumroad (Recommended)

Best for: Instant downloads of ready-to-ink tattoo designs.

Why Gumroad?

  • Safe, fast, and easy checkout.
  • Instant access to your purchased files after payment.
  • Support for major credit/debit cards and Apple Pay / Google Pay.

How to pay with Gumroad:

  1. Browse our designs and click “Buy Now”.
  2. You’ll be redirected to Gumroad’s secure checkout page.
  3. Fill in your payment details and complete the order.
  4. Once payment is complete, your digital file will be available immediately for download.

✨ No account required. Gumroad handles all payments securely.


PayPal (For Custom Orders)

Best for: Personalized tattoo design commissions and special licenses.

Why PayPal?

  • Widely trusted and secure.
  • Easy international payments.
  • Protection for both buyers and sellers.

How to pay with PayPal:

  1. Click on Custom page and contact us via email: laninkstudio@outlook.com
  2. Once we confirm your design details, we’ll send you a PayPal invoice.
  3. Click the invoice link, log in to PayPal, and complete the payment.
  4. We’ll begin your design after payment is confirmed.

✨ You’ll receive a confirmation email with your order details.


Payment & Security

  • All transactions are processed through trusted third-party platforms (Gumroad / PayPal).
  • We do not store your credit card or PayPal information.
  • Prices are displayed in USD, but both platforms support international currencies.
