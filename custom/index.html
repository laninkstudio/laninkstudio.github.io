Custom Order

How to request a custom order

Every tattoo tells a story. A custom calligraphy design allows you to express your unique meaning through elegant, hand-written Chinese characters — designed just for you.

1. Download the Custom Design Form

Click the button below to download the request form (.docx).

Download here:👉Custom Request Form

2. Fill Out Your Information

Please provide details such as:

  • The meaning, word, or phrase you want to express or translate
  • Any design preferences (style, feeling, layout, number of characters)
  • Optional references or inspirations
  • Your name & contact information

The more details you provide, the faster I can give you a clear quote and timeline.


3. Send Me Your Request

Email the completed form to:

📧 laninkstudio@outlook.com

Once I receive your request, I’ll review it carefully and reply with:

  • A design concept based on your idea
  • Estimated price and timeline
  • Any additional questions to make sure we get it just right

4. Finalize & Begin Design

Once I confirm all details and payment, I’ll begin hand-writing your unique calligraphy design.

Your tattoo-ready file will be delivered via email, which includes:

  • Your-tattoo-design.png → white background, black line art
  • Your-tattoo-design.png → transparent background, black line art
  • Your-tattoo-design-meaning.txt → general meaning or cultural background
  • Your-tattoo-design-mockup.png → example on skin for reference (optional)

Why Choose a Custom Design

  • 100% handwritten Chinese calligraphy by an experienced artist
  • One-of-a-kind design — no templates, no AI fonts
  • Tailored to your personal story and style
  • High-resolution tattoo artist–ready files

Your story deserves more than just a word — it deserves art.
Let’s create something meaningful together.
