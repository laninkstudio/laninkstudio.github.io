3. Think About Placement and Layout

The placement of your tattoo dictates how the calligraphy should be composed. A design meant for a spine looks very different from one meant for a wrist.

  • Vertical Flow: Traditional Chinese is often written vertically. This is perfect for spine tattoos, ribs, or the forearm.
  • Horizontal Flow: Modern Chinese is read left-to-right (or historically right-to-left for signs). This works well for the collarbone, chest, or waist tattoos.
