"自由" – Freedom | Handwritten Chinese Calligraphy Tattoo Design
  • Symbolizes freedom, independence, and authenticity — the courage to live life on your own terms.
  • The phrase 自由 (zìyóu) literally combines two ideas: 自 ("self") and 由 ("from" or "by"), together meaning "coming from oneself" — the essence of true freedom born from within.
  • In Chinese philosophy, 自由 is not only political or physical liberty but also spiritual liberation — being free from fear, ego, or external control.
