The Origin of This Phrase

社恐, short for 社交恐惧, literally means "social fear" and has become a humorous, widely recognized term in Chinese internet culture. It’s often used in memes and conversations to playfully acknowledge social awkwardness, introverted tendencies, or the universal discomfort of human interaction - turning anxiety into a shared, relatable joke.
