The Origin of This Phrase

奶茶续命 ("milk tea for survival") is a playful internet phrase widely used in Chinese social media to express how essential bubble tea is to daily life. Beyond the humor, it reflects the simple joys and small rituals that keep us going - a cup of sweetness that makes life a little more bearable.
